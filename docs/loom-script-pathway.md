# Loom script — GrowthAI intro walkthrough (Pathway)

**Goal:** a ~3–4 min guided tour so a new Pathway user understands every screen.
**Tone:** friendly, confident, fast. Don't read this verbatim — use it as a map.
**Before recording:** log in as a Pathway user (so the data/branding is theirs), close
extra tabs, set zoom to 100%, and have the sidebar visible.

---

### 0. Intro (15s) — on the Dashboard
> "Hi! Welcome to GrowthAI — your outbound growth platform. In the next couple of
> minutes I'll walk you through every screen so you know exactly where everything lives.
> Let's start at the top."

Point at the **sidebar** (left) and the **top bar** (search ⌘K, the help "?" button,
notifications bell, settings, your profile).

---

### 1. Dashboard (30s) — `/`
> "This is your home base. At a glance you get your key numbers, what happened today,
> and a 30-day performance overview — replies, positives, calls. It's the first thing
> you check each morning."

Scroll the dashboard slowly so the KPI cards + chart are visible.

---

### 2. Company Bio (20s) — `/company-bios`
> "Company Bio is your profile and positioning. This matters more than it looks: it's the
> context the AI uses to write every single message. The better this is, the sharper your
> outreach sounds."

---

### 3. Lead Miner™ (30s) — `/icp`
> "Lead Miner is where you define your Ideal Customer Profiles — your 'tickets'. Each ICP
> describes who you want to reach, and from here we mine matching leads to feed into your
> campaigns."

Open one ICP to show the criteria.

---

### 4. Outreach Flow™ (40s) — `/campaigns`
> "Outreach Flow is the engine. Each flow is a multi-step sequence across LinkedIn and email
> — connection request, follow-ups, the works. The AI personalizes every message per lead
> using their profile, company and recent activity, so it never feels like a blast."

Open a campaign → show the **Metrics tab** (funnel, conversion, per-step).
> "Inside each flow you've got a full funnel: who was contacted, who accepted, who replied,
> and where people drop off."

---

### 5. Leads (30s) — `/leads`
> "Leads is your whole pipeline in one table — status, score, and a complete activity
> timeline for every contact. You can filter, search, and click into anyone to see every
> touch we've had with them."

Click one lead → show the detail timeline.

---

### 6. Notifications / Queue (30s) — `/queue`
> "This is your daily to-do list. Three things land here: replies that need a human,
> calls to make, and campaign steps waiting for your approval. If you only open one screen
> a day, make it this one."

Point at the tabs (Replies / Pending calls / Reviews) and the Inbox.

---

### 7. Inbox (20s) — `/queue?tab=inbox`
> "The Inbox merges every conversation — LinkedIn and email — into one thread view, so you
> reply from the same place no matter the channel."

---

### 8. Results (15s) — `/results`
> "Results rolls up campaign performance — what's working, who's converting, where to double
> down."

---

### 9. Settings + Help (20s)
> "Up top: Settings for your theme, language and password. And see this question-mark button?
> That opens this same guide any time — with this video and a one-line explainer for every
> screen. So you're never stuck."

Click the **"?" help button** to show the Help menu opening.

---

### 10. Close (10s)
> "That's the tour. Start in Notifications each day, let the flows do the heavy lifting, and
> reach out to your SWL contact if you need anything. Thanks for watching — let's grow."

---

**After recording:** grab the Loom **share** url (`https://www.loom.com/share/<id>`) for the
email template, and the **embed** url (`https://www.loom.com/embed/<id>`) to drop into
`components/HelpMenu.tsx` → `INTRO_VIDEO_EMBED_URL`.
